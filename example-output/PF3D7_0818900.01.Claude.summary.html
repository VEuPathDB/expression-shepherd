This gene exhibits exceptionally high expression levels throughout the Plasmodium falciparum intraerythrocytic developmental cycle, consistently ranking in the 99th percentile or higher across multiple independent studies. The gene shows strong strand-specific transcription with minimal antisense expression and maintains high expression across different parasite strains and clinical isolates.
