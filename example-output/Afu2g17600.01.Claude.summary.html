Afu2g17600 - ACE1-regulated gene shows developmental stage-specific expression with strong iron and hypoxia responses

This gene exhibits dramatic developmental regulation, with highest expression in dormant conidia (68.1 TPM) compared to germinated forms, suggesting a role in maintaining dormancy. Expression is strongly controlled by the ACE1 transcription factor, showing a 60-fold reduction in deletion mutants, and responds robustly to environmental stresses.
