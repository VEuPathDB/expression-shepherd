PF3D7_1342500 - Dynamic Expression of a Key Gene in Plasmodium falciparum Across Developmental Stages and Conditions

This gene in Plasmodium falciparum demonstrates varied expression patterns across different life stages and environmental conditions, highlighting its potential roles in both developmental and adaptive processes. Peak expression occurs during the schizont stage and in sporozoites, specifically in salivary gland sporozoites, which suggests critical functions in the parasite's life cycle progression and mosquito stage adaptation. Additionally, the gene shows differential expression across various host responses, such as drug resistance and heat shock, indicating its adaptability and regulatory complexity within the parasite's lifecycle.
