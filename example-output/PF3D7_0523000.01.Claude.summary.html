This gene exhibits highly dynamic expression throughout the Plasmodium falciparum life cycle, with peak expression consistently observed during early ring stages and merozoites (reaching >2000 TPM, 96th-99th percentile) followed by dramatic decline through trophozoite and schizont stages.
