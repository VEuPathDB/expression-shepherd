Tb927.3.3300 - Bloodstream form-preferential expression with S-phase cell cycle regulation

This gene demonstrates strong life cycle stage-specific expression in Trypanosoma brucei, showing consistently higher expression in bloodstream forms compared to procyclic forms across multiple experimental conditions.

  • Peak expression occurs during S-phase of the cell cycle (~1.7-fold increase over G1 phase)
  • Expression is dramatically elevated in adipose tissue-derived parasites (~5-fold higher than standard bloodstream forms)
  • Enhanced expression in human cerebrospinal fluid samples suggests potential role in CNS invasion
